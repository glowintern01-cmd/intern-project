Our 500-Hour Experience

Throughout the internship, we worked on hands-on projects, improved our technical skills, collaborated as a team, and learned how to build professional-grade solutions. From planning and design to development and presentation, every hour contributed to our growth.

Internship Highlights

Start of OJT

On January 12, our supervisors introduced us to the entire team.

Week 1

We focused on hardware management, including disassembling a PC unit, installing an operating system, troubleshooting issues, and optimizing performance.

Week 2

We dove into automation workflows using GHL and provided computer maintenance for agents. Our tasks included setting up GHL workflows, creating social media graphics, editing webpages and hiring posts. We also set up and rearranged 4 computers for the team.

Week 3

We handled several hardware and system upgrades. We installed new motherboards, repaired 4 CPU fans, and troubleshooted various PC issues. We also set up remote sharing by connecting PCs to the server using Windows, upgraded multiple units from Windows 10 to Windows 11, configured Windows Server, and set up PCs for incoming trainees.

Week 4

This week, we set up trainee email accounts for Gmail, Asana, and MS Teams, integrated with GHL websites. We made updates to the official globalofficeworks website, reviewed pages and links, and inspected headphones and cameras. We also updated CTA links, published all changes, and worked on SOP documentation.

Week 5

This week, we explored the backend for GHL automation and worked on optimizing existing automation workflows. We conducted research on SEO and drafted meta titles and descriptions for the active website pages. We also tested 3 file sharing applications, performed hardware checks on PCs, and ensured all systems were running smoothly.

Week 6

This week, we bypassed a PC by removing the admin password and performed Windows updates. We standardized section spacing across the GHL Website for consistency. We also set up a laptop for remote access, retrieved one agent's PC for clean formatting, and removed existing files as part of the cleanup process.

Week 7

On Week 7 We prepared short video edits to put into the Blogs page for Global OfficeWorks Official Webpage and edited all pages through GHL(GoHighLevel)

Week 8

In Week 8, We cleaned and checked all idle PC units if they are all working or defective.

Week 9

On Week 9 We Helped Boss Leo on transferring the PC Units to the other office because of Air Condition Problems.

Week 10

On Week 10 New Office Chairs arrived and we helped assembled them, We made sure to log them into the Inventory Sheet.

Week 11

Week 11, We Checked all trainees and employees PC's to ensure the optimization to maximize performace on the Computers.

Week 12

Our last Week with Glow, We Thank Sir Joe Ada the COO of Global OfficeWorks for accepting us as Interns for the company, To our Supervisors Thank You for guiding and training us with not only on Hardware but also in GHL and Website Development. We really appreciate it! Thank you! Until Next Time, ASIAN COLLEGE INTERNS OUT.
